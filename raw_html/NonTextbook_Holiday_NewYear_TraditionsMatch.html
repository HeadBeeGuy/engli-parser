BRIEF OUTLINE: Students match countries with their New Year's traditions.  

 

MATERIALS NEEDED:

  • New Year's Traditions Matching 
  • Pictures, videos, or explanations of the traditions.

 

DETAILED EXPLANATION:

  1. Hand out worksheet
  2. Review traditions and country names, but do not give away the tradition's country.
  3. Have the students attempt to match up the traditions with the correct countries.    
  4. Check the answers and give explanations to each tradition. 


  • Worksheet Answers
    • Break plates: Denmark
    • Colorful underpants: Brazil
    • Kiss someone: America
    • Fire Balls: Scotland
    • Polka Dots: Philippines
    • Mistletoe: Ireland


 

VARIATIONS:

  • Ask the students which country they would enjoy the most for New Year's.
