MID-YEAR REVIEW

 

FUN FACT: This page has no fact submitted yet. Contact us if you have a Fun Fact! (Make sure to include a link to this page in your email.)  

NOTE: A public JHS in Japan is generally on a 3-term school year. This page includes activities for end of the 1st and 2nd terms. Activities for reviewing the 3rd term can be found in the Year-End Review pages: Grade 1 / Grade 2/Grade 3.

 

GRADE 01

Crossword Challenge (New Horizon): Three crossword puzzles with almost all vocabulary from the New Horizon series (grade 1: units 1-6). Students must translate and fill in the blanks to finish.

 

Describe It (New Horizon): Talk, Act, or Draw! Whichever you choose, it's sure to be fun and challenging! Describe It is an all-in-one Taboo, Charades and Pictionary game.

 

Exploding Sentences: Students draw cards from a Mystery Box and practice saying the target word and example sentence.

 

Magic Word Box (all): Students review vocab they've learned by pulling words out of a box and getting their team to guess by drawing, gesturing or using hints in English.

 

Memory Race: This is a team-race-game in which students must piece together 10 sentences that have been cut up and jumbled in envelopes, write them down, memorize and then recite the sentence to the ALT. Everyone in each team should get to read/write/speak in this activity.

 

Merry Christmas Review (New Horizon): Students review units 1-8 from New Horizon 1 while learning about Christmas.

 

Midterm Grade 1: This is a practice test for first grade JHS students for use as review for the entire first term.

 

Review Crossword (Unit: 1-2 / 5-6) (Sunshine): This is a vocabulary review in the shape of a crossword puzzle.


Total English Challenge: A variety of word search activities for reviewing the content studied on first trimester.

 

Sengoku Jidai (Sunshine): A review quiz game where students battle for territory of Japan.

 

Sentence Challenge (all):

Each student is given seperate words from a sentence. They have to work as a team to put the sentence together.

 

Speaking Test Grade 1: This is a speaking test that reviews Grade 1's Unit1-2.

 

Total English Crossword 1: (Wrkst Only by Norm Cook) A crossword with vocabulary from units 1-3 from Total English.

 

T-Puzzle: A four Parts of Learning activity that can be used to review any grammar point for any grade.
